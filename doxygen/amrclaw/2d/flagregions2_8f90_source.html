2 !
3 ! Modify array of flagged points to respect minlevels and maxlevels
4 ! specified by regions.
5 !
6 ! Second version with outer loop on regions, should be faster.
7 !
8 ! amrflags = array to be flagged with either the value
9 ! DONTFLAG (no refinement needed) or
10 ! DOFLAG (refinement desired)
11 !
12 ! On input, amrflags is already set by flagger routine using
13 ! Richardson extrapolation and/or flag2refine routine, as requested.
14 ! This routine may change flags only in cells that are (partially)
15 ! covered by one or more regions.
16 !
17 ! If any part of a grid cell is covered by one or more regions, then
18 ! refinement is *required* to at least the max of all region min_levels
19 ! and is *allowed* to at most to the max of all region max_levels.
20 !
21 ! Note that buffering is done *after* this, so additional cells may
22 ! be refined in areas covered by regions that do not allow it!
23 ! ::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::::
